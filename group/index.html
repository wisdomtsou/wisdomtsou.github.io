About US

Recently, wearable devices have become increasingly popular in our lives because of their neat features and stylish appearance. However, their tiny sizes bring about new challenges to human-device interaction such as texts input. Although some novel methods have been put forward, they possess different defects and are not applicable to deal with the problem. As a result, we propose an acoustic-based texts-entry system, i.e., EchoWrite, by which texts can be entered with a finger writing in the air without wearing any additional device.
